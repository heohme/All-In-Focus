Privacy Policy for All-In Focus

Last updated: December 23, 2025

Overview

All-In Focus is a productivity Chrome extension that helps users stay focused by combining the Pomodoro Technique with Texas Hold'em poker mechanics. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

Data Collection

All-In Focus stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Focus session data: Your focus task descriptions and session history
  • Game statistics: Your chip count, win/loss records, and poker hand history
  • Blocked websites list: The list of websites you want to block during focus sessions
  • Settings: Your preferences for blocked sites and game settings

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. No data is transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Data Usage

The extension uses your data solely to:

  • Track your focus sessions and display them to you
  • Maintain your game progress (chips, statistics)
  • Block specified websites during focus sessions
  • Provide you with session summaries and statistics

Permissions Explained

All-In Focus requests the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your session data, game statistics, and settings locally
  • alarms: To trigger timers for Pomodoro sessions and card reveals
  • declarativeNetRequest & declarativeNetRequestWithHostAccess: To block specified websites during focus sessions
  • tabs: To detect when you attempt to visit blocked sites
  • notifications: To notify you when sessions end or cards are revealed
  • offscreen: To play audio notifications
  • webNavigation: To intercept navigation to blocked sites
  • host_permissions (*://*/*): Required to block any website you choose to add to your blocked list

Third-Party Services

All-In Focus does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The extension operates entirely offline after installation.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data. All data remains on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete all extension data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension (which removes all stored data)
  • Using Chrome's extension storage management tools
  • Clearing Chrome's extension data from chrome://settings/clearBrowserData

Children's Privacy

All-In Focus does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The extension does not collect personal information from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the GitHub Issues page.
